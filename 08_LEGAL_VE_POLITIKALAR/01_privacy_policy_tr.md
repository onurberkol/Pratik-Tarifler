## Privacy Policy — Pratik Tarifler

**Pratik Tarifler** ("we", "our app") values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal data when you use our mobile application.

### 1. DATA CONTROLLER
- **Data Controller:** [Company Name / Individual]
- **Address:** [Address]
- **Email:** support@pratiktarifler.app

### 2. DATA WE COLLECT

#### 2.1. Account Information
- Email address (required)
- Name (optional)
- Profile photo (optional)

#### 2.2. In-App Data
- **Fridge photos**: Temporarily stored for AI analysis. **Auto-deleted within 24 hours.**
- **Recipe favorites and pantry contents**: User preferences.
- **Recipe ratings and reviews**: For community.
- **App interactions**: Viewed recipes, searches, etc.

#### 2.3. Automatically Collected Data
- Device type, OS, app version
- Anonymous session durations
- Crash reports (Sentry)
- Anonymous usage statistics

#### 2.4. Payment Information
- Payments processed by **Apple, Google, and Stripe**.
- We NEVER see or store your credit card details.
- We only store subscription status (active/inactive).

### 3. HOW WE USE DATA

- ✅ Create and manage your account
- ✅ Provide personalized recipe recommendations
- ✅ Analyze fridge photos to identify ingredients
- ✅ Fulfill legal obligations
- ✅ Improve service quality (anonymous analysis)
- ✅ Provide customer support

### 4. WHO WE SHARE WITH

| Third Party | What We Share | Why |
|-------------|---------------|-----|
| Google Cloud (Firebase) | Database + auth | Data storage |
| Google Cloud Vision | Fridge photos (temporary) | AI analysis |
| Apple / Google | Subscription status | Payment processing |
| Stripe (web only) | Payment info | Payment processing |
| Sentry | Crash reports | Bug fixes |
| RevenueCat | Subscription state | Subscription management |

**We NEVER share with:** Advertisers, data brokers, third-party marketers.

### 5. RETENTION PERIODS

| Data Type | Retention |
|-----------|-----------|
| Account info | Until account deletion |
| Fridge photos | **24 hours** (auto-deleted) |
| Favorites/pantry | Until account deletion |
| Crash logs | 90 days |
| Anonymous analytics | 14 months |
| Billing records | 10 years (legal requirement) |

### 6. YOUR RIGHTS (GDPR + CCPA)

- 🔍 **Information**: Learn what data we process
- 📄 **Access**: Request a copy of your data
- ✏️ **Rectification**: Correct inaccurate data
- 🗑️ **Erasure**: Delete account and all data
- ⏸️ **Restriction**: Limit certain processing
- 📤 **Portability**: Export your data
- ❌ **Object**: Object to automated decisions
- 🚫 **Do Not Sell** (CCPA): We don't sell data anyway

**Submit requests:** support@pratiktarifler.app — We respond within 30 days.

### 7. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

Our app is **not intended for users under 13**. If we discover we've unknowingly collected data from a child under 13, we delete it immediately.

### 8. COOKIES & TRACKING

We don't use cookies in our mobile app. We do use identifiers like Firebase Analytics. You can disable these in your device settings.

### 9. SECURITY

- 🔒 All data traffic encrypted via HTTPS (TLS 1.3)
- 🔐 Data protected with at-rest encryption in Firebase
- 🛡️ Regular security audits
- ⚠️ Data breach notification within 72 hours

### 10. POLICY CHANGES

If we update this policy, we notify you via email or in-app. We seek explicit consent for material changes.
