## ✅ ASO TIPS

1. **First 3 lines are king**: "🎯 Open your fridge..." This is the visible portion before users tap "more." It must hook.

2. **Use emojis**: Visual richness, scanability, App Store standout factor.

3. **Social proof later**: After 100+ reviews, add "Among the most loved cooking apps."

4. **Update release notes every version**: Apple's algorithm penalizes stale promo text.

5. **A/B test**: Use Product Page Optimization to test 3 different icon + screenshot sets.
